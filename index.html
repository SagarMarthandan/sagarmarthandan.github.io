About

Building data infrastructure. Product-minded. Engineering since 2020.

I'm Sagar — a Data / Analytics Engineer based in Berlin, Germany. I build data platforms that turn raw data into decisions.

I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Science (completed) and am pursuing an M.Sc. in Quantitative Economics. My work spans the modern data stack — from ELT pipelines with dbt, Airflow, Airbyte, and Dagster, to cloud warehousing with Snowflake, BigQuery, and Azure Databricks, to streaming with Apache Kafka and Redis.

I'm experienced in dimensional modeling, SCD Type 2, Medallion Architecture, and production-grade BI with Power BI, Superset, and Streamlit. I also build AI tooling — RAG pipelines, LangChain, FAISS vector search — and automate everything with GitHub Actions and Terraform.

I'm currently open to Working-Student, Intern, or Entry-Level roles in data engineering and analytics engineering across Germany.
